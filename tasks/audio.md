# Audio

Tasks for playing and spawning sounds.

| Task | Description |
|------|-------------|
| [Play Sound 2D](https://bsvino.github.io/statetreetools.github.io/tasks/play-sound-2d) | Plays a non-spatialized 2D sound on enter and immediately succeeds. |
| [Spawn Sound 2D](https://bsvino.github.io/statetreetools.github.io/tasks/spawn-sound-2d) | Spawns a 2D audio component and waits until the sound finishes. |
| [Play Sound At Location](https://bsvino.github.io/statetreetools.github.io/tasks/play-sound-at-location) | Plays a spatialized sound at a world location on enter and immediately succeeds. |
| [Spawn Sound At Location](https://bsvino.github.io/statetreetools.github.io/tasks/spawn-sound-at-location) | Spawns a spatialized audio component at a world location and waits until the sound finishes. |
