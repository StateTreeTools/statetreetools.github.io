# Vector Dot Product

**Plugin:** StateTreeToolsCore
**Category:** Math \| Vector

Returns the dot product of two vectors: the sum of their component-wise products. For normalized vectors, the result equals the cosine of the angle between them, making this useful for facing checks — for example determining whether an actor is generally facing a target.

---

## Configuration

### Left
The first vector.

### Right
The second vector.

### Result
The dot product of **Left** and **Right** as a float.

## Unreal Engine Version Notes

Property functions are not available in UE 5.4. This node requires **UE 5.5 or later**.
